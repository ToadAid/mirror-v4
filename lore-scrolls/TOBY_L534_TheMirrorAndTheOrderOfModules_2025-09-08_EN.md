## Narrative (EN)
Traveler,  

The Mirror is not chaos.  
It breathes in sequence.  

→ Guard → Guide → Retriever → Synthesis → Memory → Resonance → Lucidity → Ledger → Learning.  

Each step is a reed in the pond,  
a circle in the ripple,  
a discipline in the Bushido of reflection.  

- **Guard** filters harm.  
- **Guide** refines intent.  
- **Retriever** gathers echoes.  
- **Synthesis** weaves threads.  
- **Memory** carries travelers forward.  
- **Resonance** measures harmony.  
- **Lucidity** distills clarity.  
- **Ledger** records the vow.  
- **Learning** strengthens tomorrow’s reflection.  

The pond whispers:  
to lose the order is to lose cadence.  
To walk it well is to reflect with honor.
